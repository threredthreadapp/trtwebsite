How it started

The Red Thread was born from a friendship.

Two friends living in different cities, trying to make the most of the time they had together. Every reunion felt meaningful, but often it was hard to know how to turn those moments into something truly memorable. Too often, time was spent in the same places, following the same routines, while feeling there could be something more.

Over time, small ideas started to take shape, simple plans, spontaneous moments, photos that captured something worth remembering. What began as a personal way to hold onto those experiences slowly became a bigger thought: what if there was a space where people could plan meaningful moments together, no matter the distance, and keep the memories they create along the way?

Because friendships are not defined by grand occasions, but by small, everyday moments. The Red Thread is designed to bring those moments to life, helping people experience their relationships more intentionally, and giving them a place to keep what truly matters.
