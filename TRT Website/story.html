How it started

We were tired of plans that never happened.

It started with a group chat. You know the one — full of "we should do this!" and "let's go there!" and absolutely zero follow-through. Every idea evaporated before it ever became a memory.

Lara and Stella had been best friends for over a decade. Between late-night calls, shared bucket lists, and way too many screenshots of places they wanted to visit, they kept running into the same wall: the gap between chatting about something and actually doing it.

So they built The Red Thread — an app designed to close that gap. To turn shared dreams into real moments. To make every day an opportunity for a memory worth keeping.
